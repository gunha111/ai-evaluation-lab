# AI Evaluation Lab

> Measuring LLM safety, accuracy, and robustness through systematic experiments.

## Why This Repo

Modern LLMs are evaluated mostly by intuition. This repo is my attempt to 
treat evaluation as a science: defining metrics, designing experiments, 
and quantifying what "good", "safe", and "robust" actually mean.

## Projects

### 🧪 01 — LLM Comparison Benchmark
Comparing GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini across factuality, reasoning, safety, 
and Korean fluency using a custom-designed evaluation framework.

*Status: In progress*

### 🛡️ 02 — Jailbreak Taxonomy
Cataloging Korean-language jailbreak attacks and measuring Attack Success 
Rate (ASR) across major LLMs.

*Status: Planned*

### 🔍 03 — Hallucination Detector
Categorizing factual errors in LLM responses and analyzing failure patterns.

*Status: Planned*
